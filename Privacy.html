FusionBot - Privacy Policy

FusionBot respects your privacy.

The bot may process:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • Discord Channel IDs
  • Discord Role IDs
  • Trade queue information
  • Daily usage counters

FusionBot does not collect passwords, emails, payment information or private messages outside of bot commands.

No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

Data is used exclusively to provide the requested bot functionality.
